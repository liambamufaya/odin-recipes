A Cup of Sweet-Sour Shake

Immerse yourself in the savory drink that leaves you wanting more every time you drink it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 cups ice

Steps

  1. Combine heavy cream, lemon juice, sweetened condensed milk, and ice in a blender cup. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
